Here’s a clear and useful overview of India ---
## 🇮🇳 Basic Information Capital:** New Delhi Largest City:** Mumbai Population:** ~1.4+ billion (one of the most populous countries in the world) Official Languages:** Hindi & English (plus 20+ recognized regional languag Currency:** Indian Rupee (₹) ---
## 🌍 Geography India is located in **South Asia** and is the **7th largest country** by land area. It shares borders with: Pakistan China Nepal Bhutan Bangladesh Myanmar
Famous geographical features: Himalayas (world’s highest mountains) Thar Desert Ganges River ---
## 🏛️ History India has one of the world’s oldest civilizations: Indus Valley Civilization (c. 2500 BCE) British colonial rule ended with independence in **1947** Independence movement led by figures like Mahatma Gandhi ---
## 🏙️ Culture India is known for its diversity: Multiple religions: Hinduism, Islam, Christianity, Sikhism, Buddhism, Jainism Traditional clothing: Saree, Kurta Festivals: Diwali Holi Eid ---
## 🍛 Food Indian cuisine is famous worldwide: Biryani Butter Chicken Dosa ---
## 💻 Economy & Technology One of the fastest-growing major economies Strong IT sector (cities like Bangalore) Known for space achievements by ISRO ---
